Stewardship

Water, Land & Trees

Sustainable practices across every hectare we manage.

Water

Water Management

Hamdrill Enterprises strives to produce more food using less water through efficient irrigation systems and water storage. Water is a scarce resource — we work with experts on the ground to optimize usage across all operations.

Land

Land Prep & Management

Early and well-planned land preparation aids weed control in the field, which reduces herbicide usage while optimizing the land for higher yields. Our cattle herd is managed within the carrying capacity of the land.

Trees

Tree Conservation

We protect the environment by protecting trees — especially indigenous woods, which we have prohibited from being cut at our farms.
