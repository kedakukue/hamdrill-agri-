About Us

Hamdrill Enterprises

Family-owned agri-business rooted in Zimbabwe's Eastern Highlands and Mashonaland Central.

Who We Are

Our Story

Hamdrill Enterprises is a family-owned and managed agri-business dedicated to efficient, quality production of nutritious produce and meats for the Zimbabwean market and beyond. We combine hands-on farm management with modern irrigation, greenhouse horticulture, and responsible stewardship of water and land.

Where We Farm

Our Locations

Hamdrill Enterprises operates two farms in Zimbabwe.

Gwebiwood Farm

Nestled in Mashonaland Central province, 30km from Harare, Gwebiwood Farm sits at a relatively high altitude, offering pleasant year-round temperatures. Our newer site expands production capacity close to the capital.

Spring Glory Farm

Tucked away in the Eastern Highlands of Manicaland province, Spring Glory Farm is 20km outside Mutare city. This location boasts good water supply and enjoys a moderate climate with fair rainfall.
